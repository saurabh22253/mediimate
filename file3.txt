MEDIIMATE — WhatsApp Super App
Product Specification v1.0

THE CORE INSIGHT
Most health apps fail because they ask patients to change behavior. Mediimate doesn't. It meets 
patients where they already are — WhatsApp — and makes health management feel like texting a 
knowledgeable friend.

Every interaction is either:

• Proactive — Mediimate messages the patient first (reminders, check-ins, alerts)
• Reactive — Patient messages anytime, in plain language, and gets an instant intelligent 

response

PRODUCT PRINCIPLES
1. Zero dead ends — every unrecognized message gets an intelligent response, never an error.

2. Minimum inputs — never ask for information you don't need right now. Signup is 3 fields. 
Everything else is collected contextually.

3. Proactive over reactive — the product's job is to reach out first. Patients who only respond are 
retained. Patients who initiate are advocates.

4. Tier 2/3 first — voice notes, photo inputs, Kannada/Hindi responses, simple language. Never 
assume smartphone literacy.

5. Chronic care is the core — every feature exists to serve the long-term patient relationship. 
Appointments, meals, vitals, records — all feed the chronic care program and make the doctor 
relationship stickier.
