ESCALATION PROTOCOL — RUNS EVERY DAY FROM DAY 1 
------------------------------------------------- 
Below 54 mg/dL     — CRITICAL EMERGENCY: Eat sugar immediately. Call someone. Hospital 
if no improvement in 15 mins. 
54-70 mg/dL        — URGENT: Dangerously LOW. Eat 2 tsp sugar in water or banana. Sit. Don't 
drive. Reply when done. 
70-99 fasting      — NORMAL: Positive reinforcement + points awarded 
100-130 fasting    — SLIGHTLY ABOVE: Personalised diet tip 
131-250            — ELEVATED: Lifestyle reminder + points awarded for logging 
251-300            — HIGH: Drink water, avoid sweets, take medicine, log again in 2 hours 



Above 300          — VERY HIGH: Visit Dr. Suman or nearest doctor TODAY. Flagged 
automatically.
